French Omelette

This is a recipe for french omelette

French omelette is a quick dish that takes just a little bit of practice to make.
This recipe takes about 10 min and serves 1 person.

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 30g butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • A pinch of pepper

Steps:

  1. Whisk the eggs, salt and half a teaspoon of water in a bowl. Whisk until the mixture feels really liquid and the whites are completely blended in.
  2. Melt the butter in a skillet on a medium heat. Just as the butter is melted and before it starts to drizzle,
    pour in the eggs and start stiring in a circular pattern with a spatula. Be sure to lift the skillet every few seconds and shake it, while continuously
    stiring the eggs. Continue doing so until shaking the skillet doesn't level the eggs anymore.
  3. Sprinkle with a bit of pepper if you wish
  4. Using the spatula, move the runny eggs around so the mixutre has an even thickness. Remove from heat once the surface isn't runny anymore
  5. Start rolling the eggs into a cylinder, until you get to about 5cm from the opposing edge. Use the spatula to fold the flap over the cylinder.
    Slide the omelette onto the plate
